What this project do:

See those strongly affected human’s Climate Disasters during 1998-2014, using BBC & CNN news report data.

Find the most frequently affected country of each specific Climate Disaster.

Find the report date time trend, which can reflect climate change.

Why use media data, but not official climate data:

Media always smells the hot issues, which are closely related to our life. The reported Climate Disasters must already affect human’s life.

But if we use official climate data, it only tells the climate issue, we can not know how it relates to people’s life.

Data Source:

Get original data from 'Factiva - Dow Jones' database, BBC, CNN news from 1998 to 2014, in these categories:

Natural Disaster/ Environment/ Weather/ Climate Change

Data Extraction:

Climate Disaster key words, Report Date, Location

Technical Skills:

Data Mining: Use 'Natural Language Toolkit' and Stanford NER in Python to extract location in news report

Data Analysis: Python

Visualization: Python, HTML, JavaScript

What we find:

(1) News report Regional Focus:

CNN Climate Disaster reports mainly focus on United States, fewer Europe and Asia

BBC Climate Disaster reports mainly focus on Europe and Asia, fewer America

(2) What Climate Disasters are more likely to report:

Hurricane, Flood, Drought, Snowstorm, Earthquake

(3) Disaster report location distribution:

United States and China occupy top two Climate Disaster report countries, people are more likely to focus on Climate Issue of these

two countries

(4) Disaster report date distribution:

Before working on this project, I have a hypothesis that hurricane, flood and drought maybe occur frequently in summer, and by looking

at the report time trend, we can find how the climate change, but the result surprises me:

Hurricane in United States, Flood in China and Drought in China can occur almost the whole year, not only focus on several months in

summer. That is mainly because both United States and China across large range of longitude and latitude. The climate is so different from

North to South, from East to West.

But the Snowstorm in United States is regularly happens at the beginning and end of the year.
